Couture Heritage

Every ELAWI piece is created as an architectural dialogue between rich historic techniques and clean minimalist lines. We utilize low-impact, local processing methods to hand-weave materials before applying artisanal patterns.

  • 100% fine Ethiopian organic carded cotton.
  • Hand-loomed with natural pigments.
  • Hand-sewn thread border trims.

Care Instructions

To preserve the hand-applied tilet borders, embroidery threads, and organic vegetable-dyed colors, please follow these guidelines:

Dry clean only. Iron inside out using lukewarm temperature settings. Avoid bleach, aggressive stain removers, or prolonged exposure to direct blazing sunlight.
